I was always intrigued by the unfathomable potential & power of nature.
Everything that crossed my eyes seemed amazing;
the infinite skies, the flora, the fauna, the beauty of life & the technological advancement that was simplifying it.
As a greatest form of flattery, I tried to imitate many of these through art forms such as sketching & painting.
However, that didn't quench my thirst.

Growing up in an engineering household, I dug a little deeper to recreate a working model of whatever I saw; hoping, the characteristic engineering impulse would at least aid me in understanding things better.
However I ended up with more questions on the what, how, why & why not; than answers.

I was fortunate enough to find the answers to my queries in my mentors, starting with my parents, my sister, my aunt, close pals & the umpteen teachers who have guided me along the way.

This blog is a sincere token of gratitude to all my tutors who have taught me to fish in the sea of knowledge by introducing me to Physics, Mathematics & Computer Science.
And ever since, I've been absorbed in the science, that makes you feel like you are magic.
It has helped me solve problems personally & professionally by delving down to the basics, by teaching me to

"Address the root cause and not the symptoms"

Through my blogs I wish to
share my learnings, simplify technologies & problem statements and provide tips, answers & guidelines to some questions I used to seek;
All in the hope that it would help some likeminded questing soul to find answers & better their day.

Also going by the famous quote

"To live is to express oneself freely"

Bruce Lee
Via the blogs, I've expressed my views, ideas & opinions on various topics of my interest.

I seek genuine feedback on the contents of my blog. If you have any suggestions for me or my blog, feel free to reach me out on twitter or linkedin.
Your comments are much appreciated.

I sincerely hope the blog can help you as much as it helps me. Join me as we unjargon our way through this enigma of life.
